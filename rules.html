Flippy Game Rules

Objective

Match all pairs of cards on the board before time runs out. Answer trivia questions for extra points and bonuses!

Gameplay

  1. Start the Game: Choose a difficulty level (Easy, Normal, or Hard) to set your game’s time limit and challenge level. The cards will shuffle and display briefly. Memorize their positions!
  2. Flip and Match Cards: Click on two cards to flip them. If they match, they remain revealed. If not, they flip back over, and points may be deducted.
  3. Trivia Questions: Occasionally, after a match, you may receive a random trivia question. Answer it within the time limit to earn bonus points. Incorrect answers or running out of time will result in a penalty.
  4. Game Over Conditions: Win by matching all pairs of cards before time runs out. Lose if the timer reaches zero.

Completion Bonus

Earn extra points based on your remaining time:

Formula: Time Left × 10 × Difficulty Level

  • Easy: Multiplier of 1
  • Normal: Multiplier of 2
  • Hard: Multiplier of 3

How the Formula Works

The point calculation considers difficulty level, remaining time, and correct answers:

  • Base Points: Earned through matching cards and answering trivia questions.
  • Difficulty Multiplier: All points are multiplied by the difficulty level.
  • Completion Bonus: Points are added based on the remaining time when the game ends.

Example: If you answer a trivia question correctly (500 base points on Easy):

  • On Easy: 500 × 1 = 500 points
  • On Normal: 500 × 2 = 1000 points
  • On Hard: 500 × 3 = 1500 points

Winning the Game

  • Successfully match all card pairs.
  • Answer trivia questions correctly for higher scores.
  • The faster you finish, the bigger your time-based completion bonus.

Tips for Success

  • Memorize card positions quickly before they are hidden.
  • Strategically select cards to minimize mismatches.
  • Stay calm and focused during trivia questions!
